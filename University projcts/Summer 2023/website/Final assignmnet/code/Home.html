The danger of lead


Exposure to lead poses serious health risks, especially for children and pregnant women.


Even at low levels, lead can result in cognitive impairment, developmental delays, and lower IQ.


In adults, it can lead to kidney issues, high blood pressure, and increased risk of heart disease.


Because lead can be found in old paint, water pipes and contaminated soil.


Always consult professionals when dealing with potential lead sources.
